Getting Started

Welcome to the IT Discovery Framework! This comprehensive platform helps you conduct structured client assessments and generate professional recommendations.

Quick Start

  1. Choose Your Tool: Start with Quick Scope for sales conversations or full Assessments for detailed analysis
  2. Gather Information: Use our guided questionnaires to collect client requirements
  3. Generate Results: Get instant recommendations and professional reports
  4. Present to Client: Use the generated materials for proposals and planning

Framework Components

  • Quick Scope: 30-second sales scoping tool
  • Assessments: Comprehensive evaluation modules
  • ROI Calculator: Financial analysis tools
  • Documentation: Complete methodology guide

Framework Methodology

Our proven 4-phase approach ensures comprehensive discovery and actionable outcomes:

Phase 1: Discovery

Stakeholder mapping, requirements gathering, and pain point identification

Phase 2: Assessment

Risk analysis, compliance evaluation, and technical architecture review

Phase 3: Strategy

Solution design, technology selection, and implementation roadmap

Phase 4: Delivery

Professional reports, executive summaries, and next steps planning

Best Practices

Follow these guidelines for optimal results:

Assessment Preparation

  • Schedule adequate time for each assessment
  • Identify and engage key stakeholders early
  • Gather relevant documentation beforehand
  • Set clear expectations with the client

During the Assessment

  • Ask follow-up questions for clarity
  • Document assumptions and constraints
  • Focus on business outcomes, not just technology
  • Validate findings with multiple stakeholders
